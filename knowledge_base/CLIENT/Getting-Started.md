# Getting Started

Last updated: 2026-02-13

## What You Get (Nova Horizon)

1. Nova Horizon dashboard: a live control center for monitoring and controlling the market engine.
1. Optional agent endpoint: a chat API for operational guidance.

## Access Nova Horizon

1. Open `https://nova.horizonsvc.com/`
1. You will be prompted for a username and password.
1. Enter the credentials provided by your support contact.

If you see a `401 Unauthorized` error, your credentials are incorrect or expired.

## Access Agent (Optional)

1. Health check: `https://agent.horizonsvc.com/health`
1. Chat endpoint: `https://agent.horizonsvc.com/chat`

The chat endpoint is protected and requires credentials from your support contact.
