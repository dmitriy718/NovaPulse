# Troubleshooting

Last updated: 2026-02-13

## I See 401 Unauthorized

Meaning:

1. Your password is wrong or has been rotated.

Fix:

1. Request updated credentials from support.
1. If your browser cached old credentials, try one of these:
1. Open Nova Horizon in an incognito window.
1. Close all browser tabs for `nova.horizonsvc.com` and reopen.
1. Clear saved site credentials for the domain (browser settings).

## I See OFFLINE

Meaning:

1. The UI cannot reach the API.

Fix:

1. Wait 30 to 60 seconds and refresh.
1. If it persists, contact support and include your time window and what you were doing.

## I See STALE FEED

Meaning:

1. The system has detected stale market data.
1. Trading is blocked until feeds are healthy again.

Fix:

1. Wait for automatic reconnect.
1. If it persists, contact support.

## No Trades Are Happening

Common reasons:

1. You are paused or killed.
1. Safety guards are active due to stale data or spread limits.
1. Risk limits prevent trading.

What to send to support:

1. Screenshot of the status pill.
1. Symbols and approximate time.
1. Whether you pressed Pause, Resume, or Kill.
