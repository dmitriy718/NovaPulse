# Contact Support

Last updated: 2026-02-13

To resolve issues quickly, include the following in your message:

1. The page you were on: Nova Horizon dashboard or Agent.
1. Approximate time and timezone (or UTC).
1. The status shown in Nova Horizon (LIVE, PAUSED, STOPPED, STALE FEED, OFFLINE).
1. Any error code you saw (401, 502, etc).
1. Whether you pressed Pause, Resume, or Kill.
1. A screenshot of the main dashboard panels.

If you suspect unsafe behavior:

1. Press Kill.
1. Contact support immediately.
