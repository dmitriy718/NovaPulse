# FAQ

Last updated: 2026-02-13

## Is this live trading?

It depends on configuration. Ask support what mode your instance is in.

## Can I change risk limits?

Yes, but risk limits are safety-critical. Support can apply approved changes and confirm expected behavior.

## Does higher confidence mean guaranteed profit?

No. Confidence is a model signal, not a guarantee.

## What should I do if I think something is wrong?

1. Press Kill if you suspect unsafe behavior.
1. Contact support with the time window and what you observed.
